### About
   
Foz do Iguaçu, in Paraná, is one of the first most visited tourist destinations in Brazil. It has several tourist attractions such as the Iguaçu Falls, the Itaipu Hydroelectric Power Plant including the Triple Border, between Brazil, Paraguay and Argentina. Foz now also has the newest water park in southern Brazil. 
The park has the third-largest thermal beach with waves in the world and promises a lot of fun for everyone. There are 9 types of waves that reach 1.20m in height, with natural sand and a paradisiacal view.
