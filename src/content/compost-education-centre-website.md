### About

Is a non-profit organization with charitable status providing composting and ecological gardening education to residents of the Capital Regional District (CRD) and Greater Victoria, British Columbia. Our core funding is generously provided by the CRD and the City of Victoria.

### Team

I made this website as an academic project for my User Experience course at Red Academy. The members of my team were: Abdul Rafay, Sachin Srinivasa, Yuanshuo(Evelyn) You, Victor Jianto, Yasmine Ribeiro de Oliveira and me.

### Project Goals

Simplify ways to manage contents;

Offer easier access to information;

Demonstrate the environment works;

Encourage people to engage.

### UX Research

We started the project analyzing their Google Analytics account to understand who is accessing and how. With this data, we could define our priority as a type of devices, in this case, it was desktop (59,2%).

![enter image description here](/img/compost-1.png)

**Interviews**

The next step was interviewing real users and people who are enthusiastic about this field.

**Interview 01**

Anvit is an instructor on Red Academy;
Has been composting for 4 years;
Find community, read blogs and watch videos for instructions to begin;
Change shopping patterns to achieve zero waste.

**Interview 02**

Andy, Retired, Volunteer at CEC for 10 years;
Doesn’t use the website often;
Two age groups: Retired elderlies, and Mid 20s;
Reason for visiting the website: Workshop, Composting information.

**Survey for CEC Website Users**

Simultaneously with the interviews and domain research we deployed a survey for the real website users, to understand why and how they were using it, and try to get insights and pain points.

### Planning

In this second part of the project, with all the information in hand, we initiated to make our persona, user journey map, information architecture, and user flow to comprehend how the website should behave.
